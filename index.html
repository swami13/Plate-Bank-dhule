Matroshree Shakuntalabai Plate Bank

Established under the divine inspiration of Shree Swami Samarth Seva Kendra, Adhyatmik Vikas Marg, Dindori Pranit, our Plate Bank — the Matroshree Shakuntalabai Plate Bank, Dhule Shakha — is a heartfelt social and environmental initiative.

मातृश्री शकुंतलाबाई प्लेट बँक ही एक पर्यावरणस्नेही सामाजिक उपक्रम आहे. विविध कार्यक्रम, सोहळे आणि सामाजिक कार्यांसाठी पुनर्वापरयोग्य भांडी पुरवणे हा आमचा उद्देश आहे.

  • 🍽️
    Reusable stainless steel plate sets for all occasions
  • 🌿
    Zero plastic, zero disposable waste at events
  • 🙏
    Community-driven seva initiative
  • 💛
    Donation-based model — free or affordable for all
  • 🏡
    Available for villages, towns across Dhule district

📜 Rules · नियम

Plate Bank Rules & Guidelines

प्लेट बँकेचे नियम व मार्गदर्शक तत्त्वे

🔶 Booking Rules / बुकिंग नियम

  • ✅ Book at least 3 days before your event
  • ✅ Minimum 50 sets per booking
  • ✅ Valid ID and mobile number required
  • ✅ Security deposit may be collected
  • ✅ Online or in-person booking accepted

🔷 Return Rules / परत करण्याचे नियम

  • ✅ Return within 3 days of event
  • ✅ Return in washed and cleaned condition
  • ✅ Report damaged items immediately
  • ✅ Fine may be charged for late returns
  • ✅ Missing items must be compensated

🔸 Donation Rules / देणगी नियम

  • ✅ Donate plate sets to grow the bank
  • ✅ Cash donations accepted
  • ✅ In-kind donations (utensils) welcome
  • ✅ Receipt will be issued for all donations
  • ✅ Donations tax-exempt (as applicable)
